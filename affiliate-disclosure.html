Affiliate Disclosure & Editorial Integrity

Complete transparency about how Ryokome operates and maintains independence.

Summary

Ryokome participates in affiliate marketing programs, including the TravelPayouts network. This means we may earn commissions when you book travel through links on our site, at no additional cost to you. These commissions help fund our research and content creation.

Our editorial content is never influenced by these commissions. We maintain strict separation between our revenue streams and our editorial decisions.

How Affiliate Marketing Works

The Basic Model

When you click on certain links on our site and make a purchase, the merchant pays us a small commission. This comes out of their marketing budget, not from your pocket.

Cost to You

There is never an additional charge to you for using our links. Prices are identical whether you use our links or go directly to the merchant.

Our Primary Network

We primarily work with TravelPayouts, which provides access to hundreds of travel companies including airlines, hotel chains, and car rental agencies.

Commission Rates

Commissions vary by merchant and booking type, typically ranging from 1-6% of the total booking value. These rates do not influence our recommendations.

Our Affiliate Partners

We work with a select group of affiliate partners that meet our standards for traveler experience and service quality:

Aviasales / TravelPayouts

Why we partner with them: Competitive prices, wide selection, user-friendly interface, and reliable tracking.

Select Hotel Partners

Editorial Independence Guarantee

No Paid Placements

We never accept payment for reviews, rankings, or featured placements. Every recommendation is based on our genuine assessment of value to travelers.

No Commission Bias

We frequently recommend services with lower or zero commission rates when they offer better value or are more appropriate for certain types of travelers.

Negative Reviews Included

When services have significant drawbacks, we highlight them prominently, even for partners with whom we have affiliate relationships.

Alternative Options Presented

We always present multiple options when they exist, including non-affiliate alternatives when they might be better for certain travelers.

Our Editorial Process

  1. Research Phase: Content ideas come from traveler needs, not affiliate opportunities
  2. Creation Phase: Content written based on experience and research, with affiliate links added only after completion
  3. Review Phase: Multiple team members review for accuracy and bias before publication
  4. Update Phase: Regular reviews to ensure information remains current and unbiased

How We Use Commission Revenue

Transparency extends to how we use the revenue generated through affiliate commissions:

Content Creation & Research (60%)

Funding in-depth research, fact-checking, and creating comprehensive guides and tools for travelers.

Website Maintenance (25%)

Hosting costs, security updates, performance optimization, and accessibility improvements.

Future Development (15%)

Developing new tools, expanding content areas, and improving user experience based on traveler feedback.

Financial Transparency: As Ryokome grows, we plan to publish annual transparency reports detailing our revenue and expenditures.

Identifying Affiliate Links

We believe in clear labeling. Here's how to identify affiliate content on our site:

Direct Links

Links to booking services, hotel sites, or rental car companies typically include tracking parameters that identify them as coming from Ryokome.

Widgets & Tools

Interactive widgets (flight search, price maps, etc.) are provided by our affiliate partners and include tracking when you book through them.

Text References

When we mention specific booking options within articles, links to those options typically use affiliate tracking.

Non-Affiliate Links

Links to government travel advisories, airline policies, or informational resources typically do NOT use affiliate tracking.

Your Privacy & Data

When you click on affiliate links, tracking cookies are typically placed on your device. Here's what you should know:

Cookie Duration

Most affiliate tracking cookies expire within 30-90 days. This means if you click a link today and book within that window, we may receive a commission.

Data Collected

Affiliate networks typically track:

  • Click-through from our site to theirs
  • Booking completion (but NOT personal booking details)
  • Commission amount for completed bookings

They do not share your personal information with us. We only receive aggregated data about total commissions earned.

Your Control

You can clear cookies in your browser settings at any time. For more information about how we handle data, see our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.

Our Commitment to You

We commit to maintaining the highest standards of editorial integrity, regardless of financial considerations. Our primary goal is to help you travel smarter, and that will always come before revenue generation.

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or how we maintain editorial independence, please contact us. We're committed to transparency and welcome your questions.
