Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we earn commissions and our commitment to unbiased travel recommendations.

Our Affiliate Relationships

At Ryokome, we believe in transparency. This page explains how we earn revenue and how it affects the content and recommendations on our website.

TL;DR - Quick Summary

Ryokome earns commissions when you book travel through links on our site. These commissions come at no additional cost to you. We show all available options in our comparison tools, not just those from partners. We never let commissions influence our recommendations. Our goal is to help you find the best travel deals, regardless of commission rates.

1. How Affiliate Marketing Works

Ryokome participates in affiliate marketing programs, including:

  • Travelpayouts: A major travel affiliate network connecting publishers with travel companies
  • Individual partnerships: Direct relationships with specific travel providers
  • Advertising networks: Display advertising on our site

Here's how it works:

  1. You search for travel deals using our comparison tools
  2. You click on a deal that interests you
  3. You're redirected to the travel provider's website
  4. You complete your booking directly with the provider
  5. If you book, the provider pays us a commission (typically 1-10% of the booking value)

Important: The price you pay is exactly the same whether you book through our links or go directly to the provider's website. We do not add any fees or markups.

2. Our Commitment to Unbiased Recommendations

We take several steps to ensure our recommendations remain unbiased:

Comparison Tools Show All Options

Our flight, car rental, and hotel comparison tools display all available options from our data sources, not just those from affiliate partners. We sort results by price (lowest first) by default, not by commission rate.

Editorial Independence

Our travel guides, checklists, and articles are written based on genuine travel experience and research, not based on which companies pay the highest commissions.

Clear Disclosure

We clearly disclose affiliate relationships (like on this page) and include disclosure notices on pages containing affiliate links.

Regular Price Verification

We regularly check that prices shown through our affiliate links are the same as prices available directly from providers.

3. Types of Affiliate Relationships

Flight Comparisons

We work with multiple flight search providers and online travel agencies. Our comparison tool aggregates results from various sources to show you the widest selection of options.

Car Rentals

We partner with major car rental companies and comparison platforms. You'll see options from all major providers regardless of our partnership status.

Hotel Bookings

Through our hotel search widget, you can compare prices from hundreds of booking sites and hotel chains.

Travel Insurance

We may earn commissions on insurance policies purchased through our comparison tools. We recommend specific policies based on coverage and value, not commission rates.

4. What We Don't Do

No Price Manipulation

We don't inflate prices or add hidden fees. The price you see is the price you pay.

No Fake Reviews

We don't publish fake positive reviews or hide negative information about partners.

No Exclusive Deals

We don't claim to have "exclusive" deals that aren't available elsewhere.

5. How We Use Commission Revenue

Commissions help us:

  • Maintain and improve our comparison tools - Technology and data feeds cost money
  • Create high-quality content - Research, writing, and fact-checking take time
  • Keep the website free - We don't charge users for access to our tools or content
  • Cover operational costs - Hosting, security, and customer support

Without affiliate commissions, we would need to charge subscription fees or show intrusive advertising. We believe the current model provides the best balance of free access for users and sustainable operation for us.

6. User Experience Considerations

We design our affiliate links to be as unobtrusive as possible:

Clear Labeling

Links that may earn commissions are typically labeled with phrases like "Check price" or "View deal" rather than overly promotional language.

No Forced Redirects

We don't use pop-ups, forced redirects, or other intrusive methods to get clicks.

Transparent Process

When you click an affiliate link, it's clear you're going to a third-party website to complete your booking.

7. Data and Privacy

When you click an affiliate link:

  • We may share anonymous tracking data with the affiliate network (like a unique identifier to track the click)
  • We do not share personal information like your name, email, or travel details
  • The travel provider only receives your information when you choose to book with them

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

8. Our Editorial Standards

All content on Ryokome is created under these editorial standards:

Editorial Guidelines

  1. Accuracy First: We verify information from multiple sources before publishing
  2. Experience-Based: Recommendations come from actual travel experience
  3. Transparent: We disclose limitations and potential conflicts of interest
  4. User-Focused: We prioritize what's genuinely helpful for travelers
  5. Regular Updates: We review and update content regularly
