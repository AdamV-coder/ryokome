Clear Disclosure Statement

Ryokome earns commissions from travel bookings made through links on our website. This means when you click on a travel option (flight, hotel, car rental, etc.) and complete a booking, we may receive a commission from the booking platform or service provider.

Important: These commissions do not increase the price you pay. You pay exactly the same price whether you book through our links or go directly to the provider's website.

How Our Affiliate Program Works

Ryokome participates in affiliate marketing programs with various travel booking platforms and service providers. Here's how it works in practice:

1

You Search for Travel Options

Use our comparison tools to find flights, hotels, car rentals, or other travel services. We show you options from multiple providers.

2

You Click Through to Book

When you find an option you like, clicking the "View Deal" or similar button takes you to the booking platform's website through our affiliate link.

3

You Complete Your Booking

You book directly with the service provider (airline, hotel, etc.) on their website. The price you pay is identical to booking directly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I pay more when booking through Ryokome? ‚ñº

No, you pay exactly the same price. The commission comes from the service provider's marketing budget, not from additional charges to you. The price displayed is the same price you would pay if you went directly to the provider's website.

Do affiliate commissions influence your recommendations? ‚ñº

No. Our editorial team operates independently from our revenue team. We never receive higher commissions for promoting specific options, and we never accept payment for placement. Recommendations are based on objective criteria including price transparency, customer service ratings, and overall value.

What happens if I book directly instead of through your links? ‚ñº

You'll pay the same price and receive the same service. However, booking directly means Ryokome doesn't earn a commission. If you find our comparison tools and travel guidance valuable, using our affiliate links helps support our work in creating more helpful travel content.

Is this disclosure compliant with Google and FTC requirements? ‚ñº

Yes. This disclosure meets and exceeds requirements from the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) in the United States and Google's affiliate program policies. We provide clear, conspicuous disclosure about our affiliate relationships before users engage with affiliate links.

Can I opt out of affiliate tracking? ‚ñº

You can clear your browser cookies or use private browsing mode to prevent affiliate tracking. However, this means we won't receive credit if you decide to book after using our comparison tools. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.
