About Ryokome

Helping travelers find the best deals and make smarter decisions through comparison, trusted information, and years of travel experience.

Our Story & Mission

Ryokome (旅行め, meaning "travel focus" in Japanese) was born from a simple observation: Travel planning had become overwhelming. With hundreds of booking sites, conflicting information, and hidden fees, travelers were spending more time researching than enjoying their trips.

Our Journey Began with a Problem

In 2018, after planning a complex multi-country trip across Asia, I spent over 40 hours comparing flights, accommodations, transportation, and insurance. Despite this effort, I still made expensive mistakes and missed better deals that were hidden across different platforms.

That experience led to a question: Why isn't there a single place where travelers can compare all aspects of their trip and get genuinely helpful advice?

Our Core Belief

Travel should be about exploration and experiences, not about spending hours comparing prices or worrying about getting the best deal. Technology should simplify travel planning, not complicate it.

What Makes Ryokome Different

1. Comparison Without Compromise

Unlike many travel sites that push specific partners or hide better deals, we show you all available options. Our comparison tools search hundreds of providers simultaneously, giving you the complete picture.

2. Experience-Based Advice

Every guide, checklist, and article on Ryokome comes from real travel experience. Our team has collectively visited over 75 countries and learned from both wonderful experiences and costly mistakes.

3. No Hidden Agendas

We're transparent about being an affiliate site, but we never let commissions influence our recommendations. If we suggest something, it's because we genuinely believe it's the best option for travelers.

How We Work with Partners

Ryokome partners with trusted travel providers through affiliate networks like Travelpayouts. Here's how it works:

  1. Comparison: We show you prices from multiple providers
  2. Selection: You choose the option that works best for you
  3. Booking: You book directly with the provider
  4. Support: The provider handles your booking and support
  5. Commission: If you book through our links, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you

Important: We never let affiliate relationships influence which providers we show or recommend. Our comparison tools show all available options, and our guides provide unbiased advice.
