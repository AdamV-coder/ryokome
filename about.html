Our Story

Ryokome was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate travelers who noticed a gap in the online travel space. While there were plenty of booking sites and travel blogs, there was a lack of truly unbiased, practical advice focused on helping travelers make informed decisions.

How We Create Content

Our content creation process is designed to ensure accuracy, relevance, and genuine usefulness:

1

Research & Experience

All our guides begin with personal travel experience, supplemented by extensive research from credible sources like government travel advisories, airline policies, and verified user reviews.

2

Real Testing

Whenever possible, we test services ourselves or work with trusted travel partners to verify claims about prices, policies, and user experience.

3

Community Feedback

We incorporate feedback from our traveler community and update content regularly based on changing conditions, policies, and user experiences.

4

Regular Updates

Travel information becomes outdated quickly. We review and update all our content at least quarterly, with major policy changes addressed immediately.

Our Core Values

Traveler-First Approach

Every decision starts with the question: "What's best for the traveler?" If it doesn't serve travelers, we don't do it.

No AI-Generated Fluff

While we use technology tools, all our content is written and edited by humans with actual travel experience.

Balanced Recommendations

We present multiple options when they exist, explaining pros and cons so you can choose what's right for your situation.

Accessible to All

Travel shouldn't be elitist. We provide value for budget backpackers and luxury travelers alike.

How We Fund Ryokome

We believe in complete transparency about our business model:

Affiliate Commissions

When you book through our links to partners like Aviasales (TravelPayouts network), we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. These commissions help fund our research, content creation, and website maintenance.

What We Don't Do:

  • We don't accept payments for positive reviews or rankings
  • We don't sell your personal data to third parties
  • We don't promote services we haven't thoroughly researched
  • We don't have "sponsored content" disguised as impartial advice

Our Affiliate Disclosure provides complete details about our partnerships and how they work.
