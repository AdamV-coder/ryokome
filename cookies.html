Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Ryokome uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions. Cookies can be set by the website you're visiting (first-party cookies) or by third parties whose services are used on that website (third-party cookies).

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website, understand how visitors use our site, and support our business operations. The cookies we use fall into several categories:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. The website cannot function properly without these cookies.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This includes data about which pages are visited most often, how long visitors stay on pages, and any error messages encountered. This information helps us improve how our website works.

Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make, such as your preferred language or region, to provide enhanced and more personalized features. They may also be used to remember changes you've made to text size, fonts, and other customizable parts of the website.

Advertising and Affiliate Cookies

We work with affiliate partners and advertising networks that may place cookies on your device. These cookies help track conversions and attribute bookings to our referrals. They may also be used to deliver relevant advertisements and limit the number of times you see an ad.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website includes widgets and services from third-party providers, including travel search tools from Aviasales and Travelpayouts. These third parties may set their own cookies on your device for purposes such as:

  • Processing search queries and displaying results
  • Tracking booking conversions
  • Providing analytics about widget usage
  • Delivering personalized content and advertisements

We do not control these third-party cookies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of our third-party partners to understand their cookie practices.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly.

Browser Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Here are links to cookie management instructions for popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features, particularly the search widgets and comparison tools, may not work correctly without cookies enabled. Additionally, we may not be able to remember your preferences or provide personalized content.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through the information provided on our About page.
