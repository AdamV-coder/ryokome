Privacy Policy

How Ryokome collects, uses, and protects your information

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Ryokome ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website at ryokome.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Currently, Ryokome does not collect personal information directly from visitors. We do not have user accounts, newsletter signups, or contact forms that require you to provide personal data. If we add such features in the future, we will update this policy accordingly.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on pages, links clicked, and general navigation patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and screen resolution
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location based on IP address (country/city level)
  • Referral Information: The website or search engine that referred you to our Site

We collect this information using cookies and similar tracking technologies. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information about cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use automatically collected information for the following purposes:

  • Site Operation: To provide and maintain the functionality of our Site
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our Site and identify areas for improvement
  • Content Optimization: To determine which content is most helpful and create more of what visitors need
  • Technical Troubleshooting: To diagnose and fix technical problems
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

We do not use your information to build detailed personal profiles or track you across other websites for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

Affiliate Partners

Our Site includes booking widgets and search tools provided by affiliate partners including TravelPayouts and Aviasales. When you interact with these widgets:

  • The affiliate partner may collect information about your search queries and booking behavior
  • Cookies may be placed on your device by the affiliate partner
  • The affiliate partner's privacy policy governs their data collection and use
  • We do not have control over or access to data collected by these third parties

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services like Google Analytics to understand Site usage. These services collect information about your visit and aggregate it with data from other users to provide us with insights. Analytics providers have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data.

Hosting and Infrastructure

Our Site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect certain information about visitors for security and performance purposes. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to their data collection.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With companies that help us operate our Site (hosting, analytics, security)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to respond to legal processes
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Any information shared with service providers is limited to what's necessary for them to provide services, and they are required to protect the information.

Data Retention

We retain automatically collected information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Analytics data is typically retained for up to 26 months.

Your Rights and Choices

Cookie Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect Site functionality. See our Cookie Policy for more information.

Do Not Track

Some browsers support Do Not Track signals. Currently, there is no industry standard for responding to these signals, and our Site does not respond to Do Not Track requests.

Location Data

You can prevent us from collecting approximate location data by using browser privacy features or VPN services, though this may affect location-based functionality on our Site.

International Users

Our Site is operated from the United States. If you access the Site from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our Site, you consent to this transfer.

We take reasonable steps to ensure your information is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy, but please be aware that data protection laws in the United States may differ from those in your country.

Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information collected through our Site from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

If we make material changes that significantly affect your rights, we will provide notice through the Site or other appropriate means. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the methods provided on our Site.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

Given that we collect minimal information and do not maintain user accounts, there is limited personal information we could provide or delete. However, if you have questions about your rights, please contact us.

European Users - GDPR Rights

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us. We will respond to your request within the timeframes required by applicable law.
