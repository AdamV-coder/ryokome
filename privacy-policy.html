Ryokome Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Ryokome ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: When you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we collect your email address and name.
  • Travel Preferences: When you use our comparison tools, we may collect travel dates, destinations, and preferences.
  • Communication: When you contact us, we collect the contents of your message and contact information.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, buttons clicked, and other interaction data.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and IP address.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

Information from Third Parties

  • Affiliate Networks: When you book through our links, we may receive confirmation of the booking (without personal details).
  • Analytics Providers: We use services like Google Analytics to understand how our site is used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our travel comparison services
  • Personalize your experience on our website
  • Send newsletters and travel updates (with your consent)
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Analyze website usage to improve our content and tools
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal information based on:

  • Consent: When you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Legitimate Interests: To provide and improve our services
  • Contractual Necessity: When you use our comparison tools
  • Legal Obligation: When required by law

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

Service Providers

  • Email marketing platforms (for newsletters)
  • Analytics providers (to understand website usage)
  • Hosting providers (to operate our website)

Affiliate Partners

When you click on a travel deal and book with a partner, you are providing information directly to that partner. Their privacy policy will apply to any information you provide to them.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Website usage data: Up to 26 months
  • Contact inquiries: Up to 3 years for record-keeping

6. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction

You can request access to the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it's inaccurate.

Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.

Marketing Communications

You can unsubscribe from our newsletters at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email.

Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. See our Cookie Policy for more information.

Do Not Track

Our website does not respond to Do Not Track signals. However, you can control tracking through cookie settings.

7. International Data Transfers

We are based in the United States, and your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. When we transfer personal information from the EEA or UK, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party travel providers. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when changes were made. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

13. Additional Regional Disclosures

California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about them
  • Delete personal information collected from them
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Nevada Residents

Nevada residents may opt out of the sale of certain personal information. We do not sell your personal information as defined under Nevada law.

European Economic Area and UK Residents

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Summary for Users

We collect minimal personal information to provide travel comparison services. We don't sell your data. You control your newsletter subscriptions. We use cookies to improve our website. When you book travel through our links, you're dealing directly with the travel provider.
