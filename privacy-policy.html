Privacy Policy

Transparent information about how Ryokome handles your data, cookies, third-party services, and your privacy rights. This policy complies with GDPR requirements and other applicable privacy regulations.

Last updated: December 2023

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Ryokome is committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Important: No Direct Booking on Ryokome

Ryokome is a travel comparison and guidance platform. We do not process bookings directly. When you decide to book travel services, you will be redirected to third-party booking platforms (airlines, hotels, car rental companies, etc.) who will collect and process your booking information according to their own privacy policies.

Key Definitions

Personal Data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
Processing
Any operation performed on personal data, such as collection, storage, or analysis.
Data Controller
Ryokome, which determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
Third-Party Services
External platforms like Aviasales, Travelpayouts, and booking providers that process data when you book through our platform.

1. What Data We Collect

Ryokome collects minimal data necessary to provide our comparison services and improve user experience:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, device information, operating system, and approximate location (country/city level for currency and language settings).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, search queries entered, interaction with comparison widgets, and referral sources.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies to remember preferences, analyze traffic patterns, and support affiliate tracking. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information.
  • Voluntary Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and the content of your message to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, or sexual orientation) unless voluntarily provided by you in communications.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. We use different types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality, such as remembering your cookie preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website through anonymous statistical data.
  • Affiliate Tracking Cookies: Used by our third-party partners (like Aviasales and Travelpayouts) to track referrals and ensure proper commission tracking when you book through our links.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings, such as currency and language preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. However, disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

Ryokome integrates with several third-party services to provide comparison functionality:

  • Aviasales/Travelpayouts: Our flight comparison widgets are powered by Travelpayouts, which processes search data and tracks referrals. When you click on flight options, you are redirected to Aviasales or airline websites.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand website traffic and user behavior. Data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Cloudflare: Provides security and content delivery services, processing technical data to protect our website.
  • Booking Platforms: When you proceed to book travel services, you leave Ryokome and interact directly with booking platforms (airlines, hotels, car rental companies, etc.).

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing how they process your data. We recommend reviewing their policies before providing personal information.

Important: Ryokome does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data necessary for the functioning of our comparison services or when required by law.

4. Legal Basis and Purpose of Processing

Under GDPR and similar regulations, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Legitimate Interest: Processing technical and usage data to provide, secure, and improve our services, analyze traffic patterns, and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and analytics, where required by law.
  • Contractual Necessity: If you contact us, processing your information to respond to your inquiries.
  • Legal Obligation: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.

We process your data for specific, limited purposes: providing travel comparison services, improving user experience, maintaining website security, complying with legal obligations, and communicating with users who contact us.

5. Data Retention Periods

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Technical and Usage Data: Up to 26 months for analytics purposes.
  • Communication Data: Up to 3 years after last contact, unless longer retention is required for legal reasons.
  • Cookie Data: Varies by cookie type, from session duration to up to 2 years. See our Cookie Policy for specific durations.
  • Backup Data: Regular backups are retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes.

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized for statistical purposes.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we process your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where consent was the legal basis.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days, though this period may be extended for complex requests.

7. International Data Transfers

Ryokome operates globally, and your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. These countries may have different data protection laws.

When we transfer data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, to ensure your data receives adequate protection.

Third-party services we use (like Google Analytics and Travelpayouts) may also transfer data internationally in accordance with their own policies and safeguards.

8. Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Regular security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Secure data center facilities with physical protections
  • Regular security awareness training for our team

While we implement robust security measures, no online service can guarantee absolute security. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your login credentials confidential.

9. Children's Privacy

Ryokome is not directed to children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately. If we learn we have collected personal data from a child under 16, we will delete that information promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
