Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ryokome compare flight prices?

We search hundreds of travel sites, airlines, and booking platforms to find you the best flight deals. Our comparison tools show you all available options in one place, so you can choose what's best for your needs.

Is there a fee to use Ryokome?

No, our service is completely free. We earn commissions when you book through our links, at no extra cost to you. The prices you see are the same as if you went directly to the travel site.

How do I book through Ryokome?

Simply use our search tools to find your travel options. When you find what you want, click the link to be taken directly to the booking site. Your booking is made securely with the travel provider.

Are my bookings secure?

Yes. When you click through to book, you're taken directly to the official booking site of the airline, hotel, or car rental company. We don't handle any payments or personal information directly.
