The Complete Travel Checklist

Everything you need to prepare for a smooth, stress-free trip. Created by experienced travelers, updated regularly.

How to Use This Checklist

This comprehensive checklist covers everything from documents to packing. Use it in stages:

  1. 4+ weeks before travel: Review Sections 1-3 (Documents, Health, Money)
  2. 1-2 weeks before: Complete Section 4 (Home Preparation)
  3. 2-3 days before: Pack using Section 5 (Packing List)
  4. Day of travel: Use Section 6 (Last-Minute Checks)

Check items as you complete them. The checklist will save your progress automatically.

üéØ Specialized Checklists

Additional considerations for specific types of travel.

Business Travel

  • Business cards
  • Presentation materials (on USB and cloud backup)
  • Professional attire
  • Company credit card
  • Meeting schedules and contacts
  • Expense tracking system/app

Traveling with Kids

  • Child's passport/ID
  • Notarized permission if traveling with one parent
  • Snacks, toys, activities for travel
  • Child medications/dosages
  • Stroller/car seat (check airline policies)
  • Baby wipes, diapers, changing pad

Adventure Travel

  • Proper footwear for activities
  • Technical clothing (quick-dry, layers)
  • Waterproof bags for electronics
  • Headlamp/flashlight
  • Multi-tool (check airline rules)
  • Map/compass/GPS device

Beach/Tropical

  • Beach towel (quick-dry recommended)
  • Water shoes
  • Snorkel gear (if not renting)
  • Beach bag
  • Aloe vera/after-sun care
  • Waterproof phone case

Final Travel Tips

  • Arrive early: International flights: 3 hours before; Domestic: 2 hours before
  • Stay hydrated: Drink water before and during your flight
  • Be flexible: Travel often involves unexpected changes
  • Keep copies separate: Store digital copies of important documents in cloud storage
  • Trust your instincts: If something feels wrong, it probably is
